What is Cybersecurity Awareness?

Cybersecurity awareness helps you understand online threats and protect your personal information effectively.

Top Safety Tips

Strong Passwords

Use unique passwords for each account and update them regularly.

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

Think Before Clicking

Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments.

Back Up Data

Regularly back up your important files to prevent data loss.
